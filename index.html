Wave To Earth

Overview

Who Are They?

Wave to Earth (웨이브투어스) is a South Korean indie-rock trio affiliated with the 'WAVY' label, a Korean Hip-Hop/R&B establishment founded by Colde. Comprising vocalist and guitarist Kim Daniel, drummer Shin Donggyu, and bassist Cha Soonjong, the band marked their debut on August 23, 2019, with their inaugural single 'wave,' subsequently followed by the EPs 'wave 0.01' and 'summer flows 0.02.' While officially a trio, additional session members such as Jo Jung-geun (keyboard), Jeon-min (saxophone) and Hong Seung-gi, an artwork member that contributes to the band's dynamic. Transitioning to the 'Wavy Seoul' label, they continued to make an impact with singles like 'nouvelle vague' and 'calla.'

Wave to Earth' crafts indie pop with a lo-fi essence rooted in jazz drumming, concocting a distinct ambiance that captures the essence of seasons and a dreamy atmosphere. Embracing the motto "All Self-made," the band takes charge of every aspect, from recording, mixing, and mastering to overseeing the broader artistic landscape, encompassing album art, videos, and fashion. The band's moniker, 'Wave to Earth,' reflects their aspiration to "become the new wave" and extend their unique universe globally.
