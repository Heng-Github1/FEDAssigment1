WaveToEarth

Musician/Band

Despite being a Korean band, Wave To Earth predominantly writes their songs in English. The group initially debuted in August 2019 with two members, Shin Donggyu and Daniel, releasing the single 'Wave.' In January 2020, they expanded their lineup with the addition of Soonjong Cha and launched their first EP, '[Wave 0.01].' The fandom name, "plankton," draws inspiration from the lyrics of their song "surf," specifically the phrase "surf like plankton."

Members

Shin Donggyu

Drummer

Donggyu is a versatile musician with a flair for jazz, he is formerly part of the jazz group called AIFF, in short for Afro Influenced Funk Federation. Beyond music, he shares his life with a small black dog named Jelmi. Having been in a band with Kim Daniel since their middle school days, Donggyu also showcases his drumming skills in the band 'Ant Is Fourmi in French' and is proficient in playing the piano.

Cha SoonJong

Bassist

Holder of a degree in Applied Music and Bass from the Seoul Institute of the Arts, SoonJong Cha brings a wealth of musical knowledge to Wave To Earth. Fluent in English, he boasts the best language skills among the band members, thanks to attending an American missionary school. SoonJong's expertise extends to mastering music, a role he has taken on from Daniel Kim within the band.
