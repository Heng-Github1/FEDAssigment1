Who are they?

Wave to Earth is a three-member South Korean indie-rock band under the Korean Hip-Hop/R&B label known as 'WAVY', which was founded by Colde. The band consists of members Kim Daniel (Vocalist, Guitarist), Shin Donggyu (Drummer), and Cha Soonjong (Bassist). They made their debut on August 23, 2019, with the release of their first single 'wave', followed by EPs 'wave 0.01' and 'summer flows 0.02'. While there are three official members, the band also includes other session members Jo Jung-geun, Jeon-min and Hong Seung-gi. After joining the 'Wavy Seoul' label, they continued to make waves with singles 'nouvelle vague' and 'calla.'

'Wave to Earth' produces indie pop with a lo-fi sound based on jazz drumming, creating a unique sense of the seasons and a dreamy atmosphere in the music. With the primary motto of "All Self-made," all recording, mixing, and mastering is done directly by the band members, who also oversee the overall art field, including album art, video, and fashion.

Their group name 'Wave to Earth' reveals the band's desire to "become the new wave" and expand its own universe across the world.

Description

On the evening of Friday, August 25, the Korean indie-rock sensation wave to earth delivered a captivating and heartwarming performance to a completely sold-out crowd at the Belasco. The anticipation leading up to the event had been building since May, when the announcement of the Seoul-based band's headline North American tour generated widespread excitement. The initial tour stops quickly sold out, prompting the tour promoter, KOHAI, to respond swiftly by upgrading venues and adding more tour dates to meet the overwhelming demand.

As the day arrived, lines wrapped around Belasco's city block, filled with eager fans, both longstanding and newly discovered. Some dedicated followers shared their journey of finding the band's music while exploring platforms like YouTube and Spotify, stumbling upon their songs in chill/R&B/indie playlists. Even avid K-Pop enthusiasts became acquainted with wave to earth's music through artists like LE SSERAFIM's Yunjin, who promoted the band's music on their platforms. The band also gained visibility on TikTok, where their songs received positive reactions and were widely used as soundtracks. Regardless of how or when individuals discovered the band, a diverse crowd came together to witness wave to earth's inaugural performance in L.A.

Given that Belasco is a general admission standing venue, attendees faced the choice of securing a prime spot in front of the stage or acquiring the highly coveted official tour merchandise. Those who opted for the former found themselves firmly planted in their chosen spots, occasionally seeking relief from the security or using improvised fans to combat the rising body heat, exacerbated by the venue's lack of air conditioning. As showtime approached, every inch of the general admission floor and balcony was filled with enthusiastic attendees ready for an unforgettable night.
