Wave To Earth Facts

  • Most of the group’s songs are written in English despite it being a Korean band.
  • Originally, Wave To Earth debuted with two members Shin Donggyu and Daniel with the single ‘Wave’ in August 2019.
  • The band released their first EP titled ‘[Wave 0.01]’, introducing their new member Soonjong Cha in January 2020.
  • Their fandom name is “plankton,” which is inspired from the lyrics of their song “surf” where the term “surf like plankton” is used.
  • The band was under the management of We Are Not 0, which is part of the POCLANOS label.
  • On November 21, 2021, it was announced that they had joined the Wavy label.
  • Their musical style can be described as indie pop with influences from jazz and lofi genres.
  • In addition to the official members, the band’s performances sometimes feature session members such as Jo Jung-geun on keyboard, Jeon-min on saxophone, and Hong Seung-gi contributing artwork.
  • The name “Wave to Earth” reflects the band’s aspiration to be a new wave in music and to extend their creative universe globally.
  • Even though the band has a fandom name, it is not commonly used by fans or by the band members.
  • The band announced their first North American tour in May 2023.
